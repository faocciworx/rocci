How we met

Rochele and Faocci’s story began back in high school, where two seemingly different paths crossed. Rochele was a quiet, boyish girl who loved singing and painting. Faocci, meanwhile, was the type who often skipped classes to spend time at the computer shop, more interested in games than grades. Though he wasn’t a troublemaker, academics weren’t his priority. Their bond was built on playful teasing. Faocci would provoke her just enough to earn a playful chase and a punch, and Rochele would always rise to the occasion with laughter. What began as innocent fun soon turned into something deeper. As the years passed, that friendship slowly bloomed into love. And now, after almost seven years of growing side by side, through life’s ups and downs, they’ve become not only each other’s partner but also each other’s greatest support. Rochele and Faocci have learned, loved, and grown together. Today, they are ready to step into the next chapter of their story. Hand in hand, heart to heart, they look forward to a lifetime of shared dreams, continued growth, and unwavering love.
